Rotherham College

Fill up on inspiration and start your journey to your dream career by studying on one of Rotherham College’s career-focused courses led by industry expert tutors.

Rotherham College has close links with employers and excellent facilities that reflect real working environments mean that you will leave college ready to achieve your career goals, whether that be in further study at university or by securing that job you’ve always dreamed of.

Rotherham College has a wide range of inspiring courses for you to choose from meaning there is something for everyone. Level 3 courses are equivalent to A-Levels and are worth UCAS points, which means you can progress onto a range of degree courses after completion. They are mostly assessed by project work over the course of the year and can involve some exams depending on the qualification.

For those eager to jump into the world of work and start their dream career, Rotherham College offers industry placements, talks, visits and live projects as part of its courses that will ensure you’re prepared for your chosen industry. There may also be opportunities to develop skills of enterprise and entrepreneurship and take part in competitions which will further enrich your CV.
