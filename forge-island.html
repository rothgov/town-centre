Forge Island

A new leisure destination within the heart of Rotherham, Forge Island is a key development in the Town Centre Masterplan, occupying a prominent river and canal side site on the edge of the Town Centre.

Forge Island maximises its waterside location and the development will bring a cinema, hotel, restaurants and bars, as well as modern residential opportunities and attractive public realm connecting to the wider town centre. The cinema partner will be announced in early 2020.

Eric Wright Civil Engineering Ltd have started work on the multi-million pound Phase One flood defence and enabling works, which are expected to be completed in spring 2020.

This central location is easily accessible to visitors from the adjacent Train Station and nearby Bus Interchange. Ample parking will also be available.
