Parking in Rotherham

Whether you live, work or shopping in Rotherham, there are a number of reasonably priced Council car parks and on street parking locations within the town centre, with free options also available.

Find parking in Rotherham

Free Saturday parking

Free all day parking on Saturdays at Wellgate Multi-Storey, Drummond Street and Forge Island car parks.

Free Sunday parking

Free all day parking on Sundays at all Council car parks (excluding Wellgate Multi-Storey)

Mid-week two hours free parking

Town centre visitors can park for free for two hours in the "red zone" at Forge Island and Drummond Street, although a ticket is needed.

Find a car park

Free electric vehicle charging

Charge your electric vehicle for free at any of the 28 charging points in Council car parks until the end of March 2020. Usual car parking fees may apply.

Further charging points can be found at South Yorkshire Passenger Transport Executive's Interchange car park.
